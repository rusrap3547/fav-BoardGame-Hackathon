🎯 Game Overview – Stratego: Solo Conquest

Stratego: Solo Conquest is a one-player strategy game where you face off against a computer-controlled opponent (AI) on a battlefield of hidden identities and tactical deception.

🧑‍🚀 Objective

Capture the enemy AI’s Flag or leave the AI with no valid moves remaining.

🧩 Game Setup

  • Players: 1 Human vs AI
  • Pieces: 80 total pieces
  • 40 Blue (Human Player)
  • 40 Red (AI Opponent)

    • Each side includes:

  • 1 Marshal (10)
  • 1 General (9)
  • 2 Colonels (8)
  • 3 Majors (7)
  • 4 Captains (6)
  • 4 Lieutenants (5)
  • 4 Sergeants (4)
  • 5 Bomb Defusers (3)
  • 8 Soldiers (2)
  • 1 Spy (1)
  • 6 Bombs (Immovable)
  • 1 Flag (Immovable)

    • 🧭 Board Layout

    Board Size: 10 x 10 (100 tiles)

    Obstacles: 2 central lakes (each 2x2 tiles) – cannot be entered or crossed

    Initial Setup:

    You (the player) place your 40 pieces freely on your back 4 rows. The AI will auto-generate a randomized strategic formation on its side.

    🕹 Gameplay & Turns

    The game is turn-based: You and the AI alternate turns.

    On your turn, you can either move or attack with one piece.

    🧍 Movement Rules

    Most pieces move 1 tile per turn – vertically or horizontally.

    No diagonal moves.

    Cannot jump over or move through other pieces.

    Cannot enter lake tiles.

    You may not move the same piece more than 5 consecutive times without pausing or changing direction.

    Special Rule – Soldier (2):

    • Soldiers can move any number of unblocked tiles in a straight line.

    • They can move and attack in the same turn.

    ⚔️ Combat Rules

    If one of your pieces is adjacent (vertically/horizontally) to an enemy piece, you can choose to attack.

    When attacking, the lower-ranked piece is removed.

    If both pieces have the same value, both are eliminated.

    If the attacker wins, they move into the defender’s space.

    If the defender wins, they stay in place.

    Combat is optional. You may choose not to attack even if you're in range.

    💣 Special Combat Rules

    Bombs: Immovable and deadly. Any piece attacking a Bomb is destroyed, except for the Defuser (3), who disarms and captures it.

    Marshal (10) vs. Spy (1):

    • The Spy can defeat the Marshal only if attacking.

    • If the Marshal attacks first, the Spy loses.

    🏁 Victory Conditions

    You win if:

    • You capture the AI’s Flag

    • The AI runs out of movable pieces

    You lose if:

    • The AI captures your Flag

    • You run out of movable pieces
